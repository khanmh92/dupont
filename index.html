about us

a little information about us

Dupont Photographers is your one-stop solution for corporate photography in the Washington DC metro area. Since 1946, the most prestigious firms in our nation's capital have relied on Dupont Photographers to help them stand out from the competition.

Our studio is conveniently located in downtown Washington. Our full-time staff is available Monday-Friday to assist with scheduling, questions, and last-minute deadlines. Need us to come to you? Our photographers will create your portraits or document events on location.

Use the links above to browse our portfolios, get directions, and read testimonials from some of our satisfied clients, then click Contact Us to book Dupont Photographers for your next professional portrait, corporate event, or group photo!
